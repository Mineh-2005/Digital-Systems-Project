Process

From CV to career in four steps

Our AI pipeline reads your profile deeply, understands your market value, and delivers actionable matches — not just a list of job titles.

Step 01
Upload Your CV
Drag and drop in PDF, DOCX, or TXT. No reformatting required — we parse it all.
Step 02
AI Profile Extraction
Our NLP engine extracts your degree, skills, and experience to build a rich semantic profile.
Step 03
Smart Job Matching
TF-IDF vectorisation and skills intersection scores every relevant job against your profile instantly.
Step 04
Act on Intelligence
Apply with confidence. Know your market value, skills gaps, and which roles are truly worth your time.
