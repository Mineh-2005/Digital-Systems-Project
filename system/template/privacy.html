Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and email address
  • CV content (education, skills, work experience)
  • Job preferences and search queries

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our job recommendation service
  • Analyze CV content to extract relevant information
  • Match your profile with suitable job opportunities
  • Communicate with you about your account and recommendations

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

5. GDPR Compliance

In accordance with GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use localStorage to store session information and user preferences. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@jobrecommender.com
